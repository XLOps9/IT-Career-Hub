Workforce Growth

The IT workforce is expanding rapidly, with job demand increasing due to the continued digital transformation of industries, emerging technologies, and the growing need for cybersecurity professionals.

Forecast: The IT job market will continue to grow at an above-average pace, with an expected 13% increase in employment from 2020 to 2030. Specialized roles in cybersecurity, AI, cloud computing, and data science are expected to see the strongest growth.

Average Age: The average age of IT workers is around 42 years, reflecting a more mature workforce with experienced professionals. However, there is a steady influx of younger workers entering the field.

Factors Impacting the Workforce: The increasing demand for specialized skills, combined with a growing emphasis on diversity, upskilling, and the global talent shortage, will shape the future of the IT workforce.

Overall, the outlook for the IT sector remains very positive, with strong growth driven by both technological innovation and increasing reliance on IT infrastructure across industries.
