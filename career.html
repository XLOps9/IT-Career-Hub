Entry-Level Positions

Typical roles include IT Support Specialist, Help Desk Technician, Junior Software Developer, and more.

Skills: Basic IT knowledge, certifications like CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+.

Salary: $50,000 - $70,000

Mid-Level Positions

Roles like Systems Administrator, Network Engineer, and Cybersecurity Analyst.

Skills: Advanced technical skills, CCNP, AWS certifications.

Salary: $70,000 - $100,000

Senior-Level Positions

Includes Senior Systems Administrator, DevOps Engineer, and Security Architect.

Skills: Leadership and specialized certifications.

Salary: $100,000 - $130,000+

Management/Executive Roles

Roles like IT Manager, IT Director, CIO, CTO.

Skills: Leadership, strategic thinking.

Salary: $120,000 - $200,000+
